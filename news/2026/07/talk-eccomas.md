**21.07.2026** **Noé** has been [nominated by the SMAI-GAMNI](http://smai.emath.fr/spip.php?article977)  to compete in the ECCOMAS PhD Olympiads at WCCM ECCOMAS 2026 in Munich, Germany.

**Noé** will give a presentation about his PhD work on [*spectral shape-optimization methods for accelerated MD algorithms*](https://noeblassel.github.io/outreach/eccomas2026.html)
