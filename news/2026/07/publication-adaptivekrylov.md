**09.07.2026: New publication**
Our article [Efficient Krylov methods for linear response in plane-wave electronic structure calculations](https://doi.org/10.1137/25M1759033) has been published today in SIAM Journal of Scientific computing.

This work is the outcome of Bonan's master thesis in the MatMat group, where he worked on inexact Krylov methods to accelerate typical density-functional perturbation theory calculations.
