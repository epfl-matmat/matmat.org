**20.04.2026 - 24.04.2026** 
The MatMat group attends the 
workshop on the [Foundations and Extensions of Density-Functional Theory](https://uni.oslomet.no/regal/workshop-foundations-and-extensions-of-dft/) in Oslo, Norway.
**Michael** will give an invited talk titled
[*Basis set error estimation in plane-wave density-functional theory*](https://michael-herbst.com/talks/2026.04.20_DFT_Error_Oslo.pdf)
and **Niklas** will speak about his recent work on [*algorithmic differentiation
for density-functional theory*](https://github.com/ERC-REGAL/REGAL/blob/main/Workshop-REGAL-Oslo-2026/Schmitz.pdf).
