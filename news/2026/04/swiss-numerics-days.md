**10.04.2026** 
This year's [Swiss Numerics Day](https://math.ethz.ch/sam/news-and-events/conferences-and-workshops/swiss-numerics-day1.html)
meeting takes place at ETH Zürich,
where **Michael** will present on
[*Kohn-Sham Density-Functional Theory: Perspectives on various inverse problems*](https://michael-herbst.com/talks/2026.04.10_Swiss_Numerics_Day.pdf).
In this presentation he discusses recent work on
[Moreau-Yosida Regularization in Density-Functional Theory](https://doi.org/10.1088/2516-1075/ae55eb)
and its use for
[inverse problems in the Kohn-Sham DFT setting](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevB.111.205143).
