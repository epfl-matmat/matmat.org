**08.12.-10.12.2025** **Niklas Schmitz** is giving a contributed talk at the [28th EuroAD workshop](https://indico.cern.ch/event/1543104/overview) at CERN. He will talk about *Algorithmic Differentiation for plane-wave DFT* based on our [recent article in npj Computational Materials](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41524-025-01880-3).
